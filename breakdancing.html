Breakdancing

Breakdown:A look at the movement, athleticism, and world-class competition that inspires my style. Breakdancing combines skill, strength, flexibility, coordination and musicality. Breakdancing is way for people all over the world to have fun, challenge themselves, and become more confident in who they are as a person. It has grown from a small group of kids in the 1970s in New York to a gobal sport.

Competiton: Competitions are between two individuals who battle in 3 short rounds each round lasting no longer than 2 minutes. A panel of judges decides who is better by holding up a name and the Bboy or Bgirl with more votes moves on to the next round.

Personal Connection

Watching these battles helps me visualize the "flow" between high-power moves and intricate footwork. I aim to bring that same level of discipline to my own sessions. I have been breakdancing for 7 years now and I love being able to let loose and dance to the music in unique ways and create something new through dance. I also love how explosive and powerful breakdacning is and I love watching professional Bboys and Bgirls compete hitting beats and doing things that seem impossible. Breakdancing has done much to imporve my self confidence and help me to be a better person better able to confront the challenges each and every day.
